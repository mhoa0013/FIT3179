Christianity in Australia

The spread of Christianity started with the colonisation of Australia and has since been a prevalent religion. However in recent times there has a decline in the population who follow the religion.

Map of Christianity in Australia

The ratio of the population that follow Christianity is consistent across all states however the number of people over the years are beginning the decline more and more.

Decline of Christianity

A closer look at the total number of people who follow Christianity in Australia shows a steady decline with the population consisting of only half of them Christian.

Generation of Christians

From this graph we can see that the although a lot of the older generation followed Christianity the younger generation are moving away from Christianity possibly because the older generation do not influence them as much.

Religions of Migrants

A possible reason could be due to migrants. A lot of migrants coming to Australia to not follow Christianity rather their own religion and as a result as their numbers grow their children would follow their religion and others may decide to follow a different religion or they marry into a non-Christian religion and convert.
